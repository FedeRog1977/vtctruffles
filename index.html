A Short Introduction

The brand was born in March of 2019 by two university students, Lewis and Saarah. The company was designed to share a love for extravagance, indulgence and premium quality through various new explorations in confectionery. If you want an original taste of our inspiration, have a look at the Signature Truffle Collection. Including some great classics, innovative desserts and some exquisite premium flavours.

“Virtuous Truffle Company”, perhaps a slightly laughable name but a fun play on the nature of the brand. It was actually inspired by a small bar and restaurant chain who promote a fair workplace and high quality production. This idea was reinforced by a love for Heston Blumenthal, who uses scientific fact to innovate and perfect. Virtuous Truffle Company takes pride in methodology, high quality ingredients, and using basic science and social surroundings to tailor products to modern trends.
